ITALIAN PIZZA

INGREDIENTS
1 cup warm water
1 packet active dry yeast
1 tablespoon sugar
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon salt
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup pizza sauce
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
1/2 cup chopped fresh oregano
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/2 cup chopped fresh rosemary
1/2 cup chopped fresh thyme
1/2 cup chopped fresh sage
1/2 cup chopped fresh marjoram
1/2 cup chopped fresh tarragon

Instructions For the Dough: Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it stand until frothy, about 5-10 minutes. Mix the Dough: In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until the dough begins to come together. Knead the Dough: Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic, about 8-10 minutes. Add more flour if necessary. Let the Dough Rise: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size. Punch Down the Dough: After the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into two equal parts. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
